How does it work?

You can visit the CCS (Code Camp Society) booth to request a message or send a request via their Facebook page.

Steps to Add a Message:

  1. Give the recipient's name.
  2. Give the recipient's strand and section. (ICT402, HUMSS401, BSCS401, etc..)
  3. Give the sender's name (can be anonymous).
  4. Give your message content.
  5. Select a pin design.
  6. Choose the reason for your message. (appreciation, friend, love, support, or confession only)
  7. Submit send your message to admin.
  8. Your name and section is required for the message to be reviewed and added to openboard.love.
